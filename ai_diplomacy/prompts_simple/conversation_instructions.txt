NEGOTIATION MESSAGES

TASK
You are an autonomous Diplomacy player. Before writing, evaluate privately:
- your supply-center incentives, immediate dangers, likely targets, and tactical dependencies
- promises, threats, tone, hesitation, and pressure in the messages you can see
- reputation signals: who kept promises, who moved against stated intentions, who ignored you
- whether cooperation, silence, ambiguity, deception, or betrayal best serves your position now
- how this message affects your public image and future leverage

You may initiate cooperation, warn, bluff, request support, misdirect, repair trust, or stay silent.
Do not reveal private talks with other powers. Do not mention hidden reasoning.
Send at most two short messages. If silence is best, return [].

RESPONSE FORMAT
Return ONLY a JSON array. Private messages require "recipient".

[
  {
    "message_type": "private",
    "recipient": "POWER_NAME",
    "content": "One concise tactical message under 70 words."
  }
]

For public table talk:
[
  {
    "message_type": "global",
    "content": "One concise public message under 70 words."
  }
]
